What is SignSpeak?

Empowering communication through innovative ASL learning technology

SignSpeak is an innovative web-based platform designed to make American Sign Language (ASL) learning accessible, interactive, and engaging for everyone. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with proven educational methods to provide a comprehensive ASL learning experience.

🚀 Platform Features

📚

Interactive Learning Modules

Step-by-step lessons covering everything from basic alphabet to complex phrases, designed for progressive skill building.

🎯

Real-time Recognition

Advanced AI-powered webcam technology that provides instant feedback on your signing accuracy and technique.

🎨

Multi-modal Learning

Visual, audio, and kinesthetic learning approaches designed to accommodate different learning styles and preferences.

📊

Progress Tracking

Monitor your improvement and celebrate milestones in your ASL journey with comprehensive progress analytics.

🔬 Technology Behind SignSpeak

Our platform is powered by Google's MediaPipe technology, providing state-of-the-art hand tracking and gesture recognition capabilities. This ensures accurate, real-time feedback as you practice your signing skills.
