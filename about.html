What is Signademy?

Signademy is an innovative web-based platform designed to make American Sign Language (ASL) learning accessible, interactive, and engaging for everyone. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with proven educational methods to provide a comprehensive ASL learning experience.

Our Platform Features

  • Interactive Learning Modules: Step-by-step lessons covering everything from basic alphabet to complex phrases
  • Real-time Recognition: Advanced AI-powered webcam technology that provides instant feedback on your signing
  • Multi-modal Learning: Visual, audio, and kinesthetic learning approaches to suit different learning styles
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your improvement and celebrate milestones in your ASL journey

Technology Behind Signademy

Our platform is powered by Google's MediaPipe technology, providing state-of-the-art hand tracking and gesture recognition capabilities. This ensures accurate, real-time feedback as you practice your signing skills.

Who We Serve

  • Students learning ASL for academic purposes
  • Parents of deaf or hard-of-hearing children
  • Healthcare professionals working with deaf communities
  • Anyone interested in expanding their communication skills
  • Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals teaching others
