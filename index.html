Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about SignSpeak

How accurate is the sign language detection?

+

Our sign language detection leverages MediaPipe's hand tracking technology, providing up to 97% accuracy for clear hand gestures in good lighting conditions. The system continues to improve as we train our models with more data.

Can I use SignSpeak on my mobile device?

+

Yes! SignSpeak is designed to be responsive and works on most modern mobile devices with a camera. For the best experience with webcam features, we recommend using Chrome or Firefox browsers.

Is SignSpeak suitable for complete beginners?

+

Absolutely! We've designed SignSpeak with beginners in mind. Start with our "Learn Alphabet" section to master the basics, then progress to more complex signs and phrases. The interactive feedback helps beginners correct their form immediately.

Does SignSpeak support sign languages other than ASL?

+

Currently, SignSpeak focuses on American Sign Language (ASL). We're working on expanding to other sign languages in future updates based on user feedback and demand.

What signs can SignSpeak currently detect?

+

Currently, SignSpeak can only detect and recognize ASL alphabet letters (A-Z). Support for numbers, common phrases, and other signs is planned for future updates as we continue to improve our recognition technology.
