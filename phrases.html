ASL relies heavily on facial expressions to convey tone, questions, and emotions. Practice matching your face to your message.

Keep your signs within a comfortable "signing space" - roughly from your waist to the top of your head.

Maintain appropriate eye contact when signing. Look at the person's face, not their hands.
