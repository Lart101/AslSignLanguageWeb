Learn American Sign Language Common Phrases

Practice essential ASL phrases with real-time feedback using a camera. Select a phrase to practice the corresponding signs.

Hello How are you? My name is Nice to meet I understand Can you help? I'm sorry Thank you What time? See you later
Hello

Open hand wave + friendly smile

How are you?

HOW + YOU with questioning expression

My name is

MY + NAME + fingerspell your name

Nice to meet you

NICE + MEET + YOU

I understand

I + UNDERSTAND with confident nod

Can you help me?

CAN + YOU + HELP + ME

I'm sorry

SORRY with sincere expression

Thank you

Touch chin, move hand forward

What time is it?

TIME + WHAT with questioning look

See you later

SEE + YOU + LATER
