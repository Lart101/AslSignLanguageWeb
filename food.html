Food & Drinks in ASL

Master essential food and drink signs in American Sign Language. These vocabulary words are perfect for restaurant visits, grocery shopping, and meal conversations.

🥗 Basic Foods

Eat
Fingertips to mouth, several small movements
💡 Universal motion of bringing food to mouth
Food
Fingertips to mouth, then down to other hand
💡 Eat motion + substance
Bread
Left hand flat, right hand slices across it
💡 Like slicing a loaf of bread
Meat
Pinch fleshy part between thumb and index finger
💡 Indicates flesh/muscle
Cheese
Palms press together and twist
💡 Like pressing cheese in a mold
Egg
Two H handshapes, crack apart motion
💡 Like cracking an egg open

🍎 Fruits & Vegetables

Apple
A handshape at corner of mouth, twist
💡 A for Apple + biting motion
Orange
C handshape at mouth, squeeze motion
💡 Like squeezing orange juice
Banana
Point up, peel down with other hand
💡 Like peeling a banana
Potato
Curved hand, fork motion on top
💡 Like stabbing a potato with fork
Tomato
T handshape + red sign
💡 T for Tomato + red color
Carrot
Fist at mouth, pretend to bite/gnaw
💡 Like rabbit eating carrot

🥤 Drinks

Drink
C handshape to mouth, tilting motion
💡 Like drinking from a cup
Water
W handshape taps chin several times
💡 W for Water at mouth area
Milk
C handshape, squeeze/milk motion
💡 Like milking a cow
Coffee
Two S handshapes, grinding motion
💡 Like grinding coffee beans
Tea
F handshape in cup, up and down motion
💡 Like dipping tea bag
Juice
J handshape at corner of mouth
💡 J for Juice + drinking motion

🍰 Desserts & Snacks

Cake
C handshape moves up from flat hand
💡 C for Cake rising from pan
Cookie
Claw hand on flat hand, twist motion
💡 Like using cookie cutter
Ice Cream
S handshape licks toward mouth twice
💡 Like licking ice cream cone
Candy
Index finger twists on cheek
💡 Sweet spot on cheek
Chocolate
C handshape circles on back of hand
💡 C for Chocolate, mixing motion
Pizza
P handshape draws triangle shape
💡 P outlining pizza slice shape

🍽️ Meals & Dining

Breakfast
Eat + morning signs combined
💡 Morning meal combination
Lunch
L handshape + eat motion
💡 L for Lunch + eating
Dinner
D handshape + eat motion
💡 D for Dinner + eating
Restaurant
R handshape at corner of mouth, both sides
💡 R for Restaurant, both corners
Kitchen
K handshape flips on flat hand
💡 K flipping like cooking
Cook
Flat hand flips on other flat hand
💡 Like flipping food while cooking

🔍 Food Sign Patterns

Common Food Motions

  • Eating: Hand to mouth movements
  • Drinking: Cup shapes tilted to mouth
  • Cooking: Stirring, flipping, mixing motions

Letter + Action Combinations

  • A + bite: Apple
  • C + squeeze: Orange, Milk
  • W + mouth: Water

💬 Restaurant & Food Conversations

🍽️ Ordering Food

Waiter: "What would you like to eat?"

Customer: "I want pizza and water, please."

🥗 Dietary Preferences

Statement: "I don't eat meat. I like vegetables and fruit."

☕ Drink Choices

Question: "Do you want coffee or tea?"

Answer: "I prefer tea, thank you."
