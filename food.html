Learn American Sign Language Food & Drinks

Practice ASL food and drink signs with real-time feedback using a camera. Select a food or drink to practice the corresponding hand sign.

Eat Drink Water Apple Bread Milk Coffee Pizza Cake Cookie
Eat

Fingertips to mouth, several small movements

Drink

C handshape to mouth, tilting motion

Water

W handshape taps chin several times

Apple

A handshape at corner of mouth, twist

Bread

Left hand flat, right hand slices across it

Milk

C handshape, squeeze/milk motion

Coffee

Two S handshapes, grinding motion

Pizza

P handshape draws triangle shape

Cake

C handshape moves up from flat hand

Cookie

Claw hand on flat hand, twist motion
