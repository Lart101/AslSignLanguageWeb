Learn American Sign Language Basic Words

Practice essential ASL words with real-time feedback using a camera. Select a word to practice the corresponding hand sign.

Hello Thank You Please Sorry Yes No Help Stop Go Me You
Hello

Open hand, wave fingers

Thank You

Touch chin, move hand forward

Please

Open hand on chest, circular motion

Sorry

Fist on chest, circular motion

Yes

Fist nods up and down

No

Index and middle finger snap shut

Help

Fist on open palm, lift together

Stop

Open hand hits other open palm

Go

Index fingers point and move forward

Me

Point to yourself

You

Point to the person
