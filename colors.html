Learn American Sign Language Colors

Practice ASL color signs with real-time feedback using a camera. Select a color to practice the corresponding hand sign.

Red Blue Yellow Green Orange Purple Pink Black White Brown Gray
Red

Index finger on lips, then move down

Blue

B handshape, shake side to side

Yellow

Y handshape, shake side to side

Green

G handshape, shake side to side

Orange

C handshape at mouth, squeeze to S

Purple

P handshape, shake side to side

Pink

P handshape, brush down chin twice

Black

Index finger across forehead

White

Open hand on chest, pull out to fingertips

Brown

B handshape, slide down side of face

Gray

Open hands, fingers pass through each other
