Frequently Asked Questions

Is Signademy really free to use?

Yes! Signademy is completely free as it was developed as an educational capstone project. Our goal is to make ASL learning accessible to everyone.

What devices and browsers are supported?

Signademy works on most modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and devices with camera access for the webcam features. Mobile devices are supported for most features.

How accurate is the sign recognition?

Our AI-powered recognition system is trained on thousands of ASL gestures and provides good accuracy for basic signs. However, it's still in development and may not be perfect for all users or lighting conditions.

Can I use Signademy for commercial purposes?

Please review our open-source license on GitHub. While the platform is free to use, commercial usage may have different terms depending on the specific components used.
