‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is Signademy really free to use?

+

Yes! Signademy is completely free as it was developed as an educational capstone project. Our goal is to make ASL learning accessible to everyone, regardless of financial circumstances.

What devices and browsers are supported?

+

Signademy works on most modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and devices with camera access for the webcam features. Mobile devices are supported for most features, though webcam functionality works best on desktop/laptop computers.

How accurate is the sign recognition?

+

Our AI-powered recognition system is trained on thousands of ASL gestures and provides good accuracy for basic signs. However, it's still in development and may not be perfect for all users or lighting conditions. We recommend using it as a learning aid alongside traditional ASL instruction.

Can I use Signademy for commercial purposes?

+

Please review our open-source license on GitHub. While the platform is free to use for educational and personal purposes, commercial usage may have different terms depending on the specific components used. Contact us for partnership opportunities.

Do you offer offline functionality?

+

Currently, Signademy requires an internet connection to function as it uses cloud-based AI models. We're exploring offline capabilities for future versions to improve accessibility.

How can I contribute to the project?

+

We welcome contributions! You can help by reporting bugs, suggesting features, improving documentation, or contributing code through our GitHub repository. We also appreciate feedback from ASL educators and deaf community members.
